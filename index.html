Welcome to the official EDUBALLsite

Eduball Academy Limited is a Coach Education Company with a mission to contribute towards nation-building efforts by providing high quality training to football coaches across Africa. Our goal is to develop the next generation of football coaches who will positively impact the game within the continent.

Get started

Why Choose Us?

When you choose us, you'll feel the benefit of 10 years' experience in the Coaching Industry. Because we know the soccer world and know how to handle it. With working tactical knowledge of athlete management, club development and Football Dynamics.

International Expertise

We understand the unique dynamics of soccer in Nigeria and have tailored our training programs to suit these needs. Our coaches are well-versed in the local and international soccer scene, providing an edge in training.

UEFA Licensed Trainers

Our team consists of highly qualified and experienced coaches who have a proven track record in nurturing talent and improving performance. They are dedicated to helping each trainee reach their full potential.

Comprehensive Training Program

Our training program covers all aspects of soccer coaching, from technical skills to tactical understanding, physical conditioning, and psychological preparation. This holistic approach ensures that our trainees are well-rounded and ready for any challenge.

Access to International Networks

Our trainees get exclusive access to our vast international networks in the soccer industry. This opens up numerous opportunities for them to work with international teams and organizations.

Global Standards

Our program is designed in line with the highest international standards. We incorporate the latest trends and techniques from around the world to ensure our trainees are at par with their global counterparts.
